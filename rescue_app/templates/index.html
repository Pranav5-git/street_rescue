About Paws & Hearts

Founded in 2025, Paws & Hearts is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming street animals across India. Our mission is to create a world where every animal is treated with compassion and respect.

We work tirelessly to provide medical care, shelter, and love to animals in need. Through our adoption programs, educational initiatives, and community outreach, we've helped thousands of animals find their forever homes.

Our Values

  • Compassion for all living beings
  • Commitment to animal welfare
  • Community engagement and education
  • Transparency in all our operations
